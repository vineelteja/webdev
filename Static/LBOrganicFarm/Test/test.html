• Pack the cow dung into a cloth sack. Soak it in water in a bucket for 12 hours. It will become softer.
  • Soak the white wash/Lime cement in a cloth sack for 12 hours separate from the cow dung.
  • Squeeze the water out of the sack of cow dung and save that water. You can recycle the cow dung as fertilizer in your home garden.
  • Mix the termite mound dirt clockwise into the dung water.
  • Now mix the cow urine clockwise and the white wash/lime cement into the dung water.
  • Leave this mixture alone for five hours.
