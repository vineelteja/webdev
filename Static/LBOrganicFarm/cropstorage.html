Crop Storage

Materials Needed

Clean the room floor with a cloth dipped into cow urine.
Take a small pot and fill it with some dry cow dung cake, dry neem leaves, and hay. Add some kerosene if needed. Light these contents and wait for the room to fill with smoke for several hours. Close all the doors and windows if necessary. This step can be repeated every 15 days during the storage period.
Now layer the ground with the dry neem leaf powder.
When layering the jute material rice bags, spread the dry neem leaf powder between each layer of the bags.
